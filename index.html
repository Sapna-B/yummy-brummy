About Us

Welcome to Yummy Brummy! We are a group of girls on a mission to help you discover new and exciting places to eat within Birmingham. Whatever cuisine you're looking for, we hope to help you discover something new or rediscover old favourites! As well as this, we're encouraging you to get in the kitchen by sharing some of our favourite recipes to help you bring the restaurant experience to the comforts of your own home!
